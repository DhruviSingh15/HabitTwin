About HabitTwin

Your AI-powered digital twin for self-improvement

Our Mission

HabitTwin was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to help people build better habits and improve their digital wellbeing through the power of AI and behavioral science.

We believe that by creating a digital twin that learns from your behavior patterns, we can provide personalized insights and motivation that truly works for you as an individual.

What is a Digital Twin?

A digital twin is a virtual representation that serves as a real-time digital counterpart of a physical object or process. In HabitTwin, your digital twin is a model of your habits, behaviors, and patterns.

As you track your habits and screen time, your digital twin learns and evolves, allowing the AI to make increasingly accurate predictions and recommendations tailored specifically to you.
