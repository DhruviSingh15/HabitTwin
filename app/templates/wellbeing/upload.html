How to Export Your Screen Time Data

For iOS Users:

  1. Go to Settings > Screen Time
  2. Tap on "See All Activity"
  3. Tap on the share icon in the top right
  4. Save as Excel and upload the file here

For Android Users:

  1. Go to Settings > Digital Wellbeing
  2. Tap on "Dashboard"
  3. Tap the three dots in the top right
  4. Select "Export data" and save as Excel

Required Excel Format:

Your Excel file should have the following columns:

  • Date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
  • App Name
  • Usage (Minutes)
